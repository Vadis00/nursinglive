Get involved with Nursing Live 2023

Nursing Live will be the first event of its kind in the UK, designed to meet the unique needs of the nursing profession, bringing both professional and personal content together in a live and vibrant environment.

A new event brought to you by RCNi

Nursing Live is an exclusive opportunity for nurses at every stage of their career to further their professional development, harness the power of holistic self-care and get access to advice and support. Nursing Live will bring nurses together for two days of learning, collaboration, entertainment and fun.

Providing partners with direct access to over 500,000 highly engaged nurses in the UK, Nursing Live offers an integrated comms approach that can deliver sector specific communications across a variety of platforms, creating opportunities to capture opinions, promote offerings and gather insights from the nursing sector.

Benefits of working with us

Partnering with Nursing Live is a real and meaningful way to support the UK nursing profession, adding value to the lives of nurses and consequently improving patient outcomes.
